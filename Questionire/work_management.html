ELEMENT 3.1

3.1 Except for emergency work, we plan maintenance work at least 24 hours prior to the work being done. The planning is done to ensure there are no foreseeable delays due to parts, materials, tools, equipment, safety requirements, information or production scheduling in the execution of the work.

Excellence

Long term planning cycles, extensive use of std. job plans. Planning is used to determine support requirements for new systems based on RCM.

Understanding
