Management and Support Systems Assessment

Excellence

Full user acceptance & widespread use of integrated management systems sharing info across org. Info widely used in EBAM, reliability, & performance management.

Competence

Extensive management systems in use with integration for sharing & reuse of important info. CBM & reliability analysis tools in place. EBAM in use.

Understanding

CMMS, EAM, or ERP in use with report generation & analysis. CBM supported with specialized systems. Documentation, financial records, maintenance, stores, etc. not integrated.

Awareness

Management systems use is spotty and provide little valuable output. CMMS is in place & operates independent of other systems. Number of ad hoc systems used.

Innocence

Little to no use of management systems. May use variety of ad hoc systems with little to no sharing of data and information among them. Maintenance is operating in own isolated information island.
